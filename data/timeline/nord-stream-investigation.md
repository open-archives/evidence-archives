## Notes
- **Germany:** Federal prosecutors filed charges in July 2026 against a former soldier accused of leading the operation.
- **Norway:** Closed their investigation in late 2022. Norway formally applied for NATO membership in May 2022; the investigation closure coincided with intensified alliance talks.
- **OSINT:** Investigations (including Bellingcat's tracking) heavily focused on the *Andromeda* yacht's movements in the Baltic Sea prior to the blasts.
- **Ukraine:** Prosecutor General’s Office officially denies state involvement in the sabotage.
- **Sweden:** Investigation concluded in 2024, no charges filed; cited lack of jurisdiction.
