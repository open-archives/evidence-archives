## Executive Summary

On January 3, 2026, U.S. special forces raided Caracas, captured President Nicolás Maduro and his wife Cilia Flores, and installed Vice President Delcy Rodríguez as interim head of state. The operation followed over two decades of U.S. efforts to destabilize Venezuela's leftist government — including sanctions, coup attempts, opposition funding, and criminal indictments.

**Key Finding:** The raid was not about democracy, drugs, or humanitarian relief. It was about **oil, geopolitical control, and regime change** — the same playbook used in Iraq, Libya, Syria, and elsewhere.
